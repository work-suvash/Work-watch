### Wiki Updates

- Added [URL Tools](https://workwatch.net/internet-tools#url-tools) section with
  subsections: [Shorteners](https://workwatch.net/internet-tools#url-shorteners),
  [Unshorteners](https://workwatch.net/internet-tools#url-unshorteners),
  [Site Checks](https://workwatch.net/internet-tools#down-site-checkers) and
  [Domain / DNS](https://workwatch.net/internet-tools#domain-dns).

- Split Search Tools into subsections:
  [Search Engines](https://workwatch.net/internet-tools#search-engines),
  [Custom Engines](https://workwatch.net/internet-tools#custom-search-engines) and
  [Google Tools](https://workwatch.net/internet-tools#google-search-tools).

- Split Multiplayer Tools into subsections:
  [Fixes](https://workwatch.net/gaming-tools#multiplayer-fixes),
  [Mods](https://workwatch.net/gaming-tools#multiplayer-mods) and
  [Servers](https://workwatch.net/gaming-tools#multiplayer-servers).

- Split Game Dev Tools into subsections:
  [Game Engines](https://workwatch.net/developer-tools#game-engines),
  [Asset Creation](https://workwatch.net/developer-tools#asset-creation) and
  [Map Creators](https://workwatch.net/developer-tools#map-creators-editors).

- Added [Office Suites](https://workwatch.net/text-tools#office-suites) section to
  Text Tools.

- Added [Wallpaper Managers](https://workwatch.net/system-tools#wallpaper-managers)
  section to Customization.

- Added [Captcha Tools](https://workwatch.net/internet-tools#captcha-tools) section
  to Internet Tools.

- Added [Tumblr Tools](https://workwatch.net/social-media-tools#tumblr-tools) section
  to Social Media Tools.

- Added [VTuber Tools](https://workwatch.net/video-tools#vtuber-tools) section to
  Video Tools.

- Added warning to Vadapav NOT to use DL managers to bulk download anymore as
  they're [banning](https://ibb.co/VvrZMXQ) for it now. You can still use DL
  managers, just download at a reasonable pace please.

- Decided to pull the plug on the feedback forum (feedback.workwatch.net), and
  instead enable the
  [forums feature](https://discord.com/blog/forum-channels-space-for-organized-conversation)
  in our [Discord](https://discord.gg/5W9QJKuPkD). We now have forums for giving
  suggestions, getting support, and testing links.
