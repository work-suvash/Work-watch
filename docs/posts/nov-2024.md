### Stars Added ⭐

- Starred [Bluesky](https://workwatch.net/social-media-tools#fediverse-tools) in Fediverse Tools as many are leaving Twitter for it now.

- Starred [Kdenlive](https://workwatch.net/video-tools#video-editors) in Video Editors. Open-source, feature-rich, multi-platform editor.

- Starred [Khinsider](https://workwatch.net/audio#game-soundtracks) in Game Soundtracks. Popular soundtrack site with a big library.

- Starred [Camo](https://workwatch.net/video-tools) in Video Tools. Allows you to use any device as a webcam.

- Starred [FMSTREAM](https://workwatch.net/storage#live-radio) in Radio Streaming. The largest online radio directory on the internet.

- Starred [Thunderbird](https://workwatch.net/mobile#android-internet) in Android Internet as it's just a better version of K-9 Mail. 

- Starred [Pixlr](https://workwatch.net/image-tools#online-editors) in Online Image Editors. Feature-rich editor with a nice UI. Has a daily limit, but it can be bypassed with the script linked.

- Starred [x266.mov](https://workwatch.net/social-media-tools#discord-tools) in Discord Tools. Allows you to embed large videos on Discord.

- Starred [Playlists.cloud](https://workwatch.net/audio#spotify-tools) in Spotify Tools. Unlimited playlist transfer between Spotify / Apple Music.

- Starred [InfiCourses](https://workwatch.net/educational#downloading) in Course DDL. Big library with courses tough to find elsewhere.

- Starred [Excalidraw](https://workwatch.net/image-tools#drawing) in Drawing Tools. Feature-rich compared to similar web apps.

- Went through [SMS Verification](https://workwatch.net/storage#sms-verification-sites) and starred ones that stood out the most.

***

### Things Removed

- Removed Mutaz as they no longer host cracked software.

- Removed Fey as the free tier is too limited now.

- Unstarred BetterDiscord as it runs poorly for many people due to bad optimization, and there are better options like Vencord.

- Unstarred OlaMovies as they now require Google logins.

- Unstarred PDALife as many apps are outdated, their official app isn't maintained, and there are better sites.

- Unstarred Not Diamond as it isn't unlimited anymore.

- Unstarred ElevenLabs as it's capped at 10 mins, limiting its usefulness.
