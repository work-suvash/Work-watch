# Stars Added ⭐

- Starred [Acer Movies](https://workwatch.net/video#drives-directories) in Drives / Directories. Big library with single-click downloads.

- Starred [Ascendara](https://workwatch.net/gaming-tools#game-launchers) in Game Launchers. Open-source, feature-rich game manager with easy in-app downloads.

- Starred [GPT1Image](https://workwatch.net/ai#image-generation) in Image Generators. Free unlimited GPT-Image-1 images.

- Starred [Axekin](https://workwatch.net/gaming#rom-sites) in ROM sites. Fast hosts, big library, and lots of platforms.

- Starred [NoPayStation](https://nopaystation.com/) in Sony ROMs. One of the best sources for Sony platform ROMs.

- Starred [1DM](https://workwatch.net/mobile#android-file-tools) in Android Download Managers as it [won in polls](https://i.ibb.co/zh2BKZ0z/image.png) and has features like split-file support.

- Starred [Metrolist](https://workwatch.net/mobile#youtube-music) in Android YouTube Music players, as people feel this stands out the most of the Outertune forks.

- Starred [AntiSplit-M](https://workwatch.net/mobile#apk-tools) in APK Tools. Easily merge split APK files.

- Starred [bleh](https://workwatch.net/audio#last-fm-tools) in Last.fm Tools. Feature-rich customization script that makes the site look much better.

- Starred [PDALife](https://workwatch.net/mobile#modded-apks) in Modded APKs. Easy download process, fast host, and good for modded games.

- Starred [GamersNexus](https://workwatch.net/misc#tech-news) in Tech News. In-depth component reviews and news for PC building.

- Starred [bt.etree, MiroPPB (ASOT), and BBC Essential](https://workwatch.net/audio#concerts-live-shows) in Concert / Live Shows. 

- Starred [NovelFire](https://workwatch.net/reading#light-novels) in Light Novels. Nice UI; allows login to rate and save progress.

- Starred [LM Studio](https://workwatch.net/ai#self-hosting-tools) in AI Self-Hosting. Useful for running models locally; supports all GPUs or runs on CPU if needed. 

- Starred [Tailscale](https://workwatch.net/privacy#vpn-tools) in VPN Tools. Secure Network VPN that's easy to use and very [feature-rich](https://i.ibb.co/ZRhQBNtX/image.png).

- Starred [Stop The Bleed](https://workwatch.net/educational#med-school) in Med School. Free first aid video courses and resources.

# Things Removed

- Unstarred G-MEH as it tends to have outdated plugins, disabled pages, and people feel there are better options.

- Unstarred SystemRequirementsLab as people don't feel it's very useful anymore.

- Unstarred Comfy Online as it has older / more limited models than other starred options.

- Removed Awesome Piracy as it's no longer maintained very well. No disrespect intended - Awesome Piracy is older than WORKWATCH and inspired us in a lot of ways early on; if it starts getting maintained better, we'll consider re-adding it.
